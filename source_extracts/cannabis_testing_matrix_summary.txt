Cannabis Testing Matrix by Sample Type
This document summarizes common cannabis product matrices and the standard analytical tests generally required across regulated cannabis markets.
Key Analytical Categories
• Potency: THC, THCA, CBD, CBDA, and minor cannabinoids• Residual Solvents: butane, propane, ethanol, pentane, etc.• Terpenes: volatile aroma compounds measured by GC-FID/MS• Pesticides: LC-MS/MS and GC-MS/MS multi-residue screening• Heavy Metals: ICP-MS screening for Pb, Cd, As, Hg
GC-FID Usage Notes
GC-FID is highly effective for terpene and residual solvent quantitation. It can also analyze cannabinoids, although HPLC is preferred because cannabinoids thermally decarboxylate in GC systems.
Typical FID Configuration
FID Instrument #1:- Headspace GC-FID for residual solventsFID Instrument #2:- Direct injection GC-FID for terpene profiling
