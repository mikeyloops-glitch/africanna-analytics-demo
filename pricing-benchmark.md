# SAGE Analytics Pricing Benchmark

Updated: 2026-05-12

This pricing note supports a middle-to-upper launch position for SAGE Analytics. The public website pricing should still be confirmed against final method validation, sample preparation cost, batch volumes, courier model, VAT treatment, and accredited scope.

## Public Benchmark Notes

- Qure South Africa listed full cannabinoid analysis at R1,000, terpene analysis from R1,000, residual solvents at R400, pesticides from R1,500 to R2,400, heavy metals at R2,200, and mycotoxins at R2,500. Qure also noted longer lead times on outsourced contaminant panels.
- Cannalytics S.A listed lower local prices for selected tests, including potency at R350, residual solvents at R200, mycotoxins at R1,800, heavy metals at R1,200, pesticides at R1,000, and terpenes at R750.
- Ecogreen Analytics does not publish a simple price list on its homepage, but it publicly positions around broad cannabis testing services and instrumentation including HPLC, GC/MS, LCMSMS, ICP-OES, and ICP-MS.
- US lab references generally place individual potency, terpene, residual solvent, pesticide, metal, and mycotoxin panels between about USD 50 and USD 250 each, with full panels often around USD 450 to USD 550 before exchange-rate, VAT, logistics, and South African market adjustments.

## Recommended Launch Position

SAGE Analytics should avoid racing to the cheapest public South African prices. The sharper positioning is fast, clean reporting, premium sample handling, and a clear full-chemistry service menu:

- Potency profile: R1,450
- Terpene profile: R1,350
- Residual solvents: R1,850
- Core release bundle: R4,250
- Pesticides + mycotoxins by LC-MS/MS: from R4,900
- Heavy metals by ICP-MS: from R2,850
- Full chemistry compliance bundle: from R10,950
